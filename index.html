I am Dr. Md Sumon Hossain, an Assistant Professor specializing in Mathematics. With a profound passion for both teaching and research, I bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to our academic community.

Academic Background

I hold a PhD in Mathematics from Rijksuniversiteit Groningen, where I delved into advanced topics such as Hybrid Systems, Optimization, and Machine Learning. My research interests span across various fields including Switched Systems and Machine Learning, where I am dedicated to exploring innovative solutions to contemporary mathematical problems.

Teaching Philosophy

As an educator, my mission is to inspire and empower the next generation of mathematicians. I believe in fostering a dynamic learning environment where students are encouraged to think critically, problem-solve creatively, and embrace the beauty of mathematical concepts.

  • Engaging lectures
  • Interactive discussions
  • Hands-on activities

Through these methods, I strive to instill in my students a deep appreciation for the elegance and significance of mathematics in our world.

Research & Contributions

Beyond the classroom, I am actively involved in collaborative research projects, publications, and academic conferences, contributing to the advancement of mathematical knowledge on both local and global scales.
